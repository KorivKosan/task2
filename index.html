Main Cyberpunk News

The long-awaited update for Cyberpunk 2077, titled "Phantom Liberty", has finally been released, bringing numerous new features and storylines to the game. Players can immerse themselves in new adventures alongside well-known characters and explore previously undiscovered areas of Night City.

"Phantom Liberty is a breath of fresh air for the Cyberpunk universe, offering an engaging storyline and improved gameplay."

Additionally, the developers have enhanced the game's performance and fixed numerous bugs, making the gaming experience even more enjoyable. The player community is actively discussing the new features and sharing their impressions.

New Cyberpunk Anime Series Announced

A new anime set in the Cyberpunk universe has been announced. This series will explore the stories of mercenaries living on the edge of Night City, battling megacorporations and their own demons. The art style is said to blend traditional anime with the gritty, neon-lit aesthetics of Cyberpunk.

The series is set to premiere in early 2025, and fans are already excited about its potential to expand the lore of Night City.
